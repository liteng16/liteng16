### 🔬 Research Interests & Vision

> **Urban Mobility & Behavioral Modeling**
> My PhD research focuses on analyzing **1M+ high-resolution GPS trajectories** to decode e-scooter and active travel patterns in Melbourne. I aim to bridge the gap between large-scale data and sustainable urban planning.

> **Spatial Intelligence & Geo-NLP**
> I leverage **Transformer-based LLMs** and NER workflows to extract mobility insights from noisy, geotagged social media data, enhancing evidence-based transport policy for local governments.
